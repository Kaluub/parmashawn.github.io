Fishing in Pixelworlds..

In Pixelworlds, fishing is one of the main ways of earning gems. Other methods of earning gems include; Mining, Blocks, and Nether. In fishing you use a rod, along with a lure, cast in a body of water to catch fish. Players can sell their fish to other players through trading, or can directly cash in their fish for gems at a fish barrel or at the Fishmonger at Pixelstation
