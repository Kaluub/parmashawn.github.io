Mining in Pixelworlds..

Mining is one of the main ways to earn gems in Pixelworlds. In Mining, a player will have a pickaxe and enter the pixelmines. Within the pixelmines a players can gather nuggets to craft keys while also mining ores to collect gemstones, which can then be cashed in for gems or traded to other players for worldlocks or byte coins. Using the keys a player crafts, it acts as a one-time pass to enter the higer tier mineshaft. This continues for 4 other mine levels, where the maximum level mineshaft is tier 5.
